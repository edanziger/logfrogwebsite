LogFrog DB Privacy Policy

(Last Updated: September 1, 2011)

LogFrog DB is a diabetes tracking application (“app”) created by Fresh Sliced News, Inc. (“LogFrog”) with powerful, innovative features patients need to manage diabetes quickly and on the go.  The privacy of our users is of paramount importance to us. Please read below regarding our privacy policy.

Information we may collect
LogFrog does not collect information that can identify you (“personal information”), such as your name and email address. LOGFROG OR FRESH SLICED NEWS, INC. HAS NO ACCESS TO THE RECORDS OR PERSONALLY IDENTIFIABLE INFORMATION YOU STORE ON YOUR IPHONE.

We may, however, collect other types of information in the following ways when you use the LogFrog app or visit our website (collectively referred to as “activity information”):

  • We use Mixpanel, an independent measurement and research company, in our app, which uses code to gather non-personal data regarding the number of LogFrog users and their frequency of use. Further information regarding Mixpanel can be found at www.mixpanel.com.
  • Our server logs may automatically collect information, such as your IP address, your browser type and language, and the date and time of your visit, which helps us track users' movements around our site and understand trends.
  • We may assign your computer one or more cookies which may collect information to facilitate access to our website and to personalize your online experience.
  • We may use standard Internet tools, such as web beacons, which collect information that tracks your use of our website and enables us to customize our services and advertisements.

How we may use information we collect 
We may use information to:

  • Fulfill your requests for products and services;
  • Offer products and services that may be of interest to you;
  • Facilitate use of our website;   
  • Analyze use of and improve our app, products and services;

With whom we may share information we collect 
We will not share information we collect with others except as indicated below, or except when we inform you in advance and give you the opportunity to opt out. We may share information with:

  • Service providers with which we may partner or which we select to offer you products, services, and promotions through our app, website or offline; and
  • Other third parties in limited circumstances, such as complying with legal requirements, preventing fraud, and protecting the safety of our users.

We may share aggregated, non-personal information in any of the above situations and also with advertisers and others.

Third Party Providers
We may, from time to time, established relationships with various providers of services and products who are not part of LogFrog and who may not themselves be providing products and services to LogFrog or on LogFrog’s behalf ("Providers").  Providers do not include third parties contracted by LogFrog to perform services on LogFrog’s behalf. The services of these Providers may be available to you from the site, from websites that are linked to the site, or the app. Typically, these Providers offer services that our users might find useful. In order to use Providers' products and services, you may be required to provide personal information to the Providers.  When you click on links on our website or through the app, you may leave our site. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of other sites, and we encourage you to read their privacy statements.  Unless expressly provided to the contrary, personal information that you provide while you are visiting a Provider's website will be subject to the privacy policy posted on that site.

Changes to our Privacy Policy
We may occasionally update our Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices and services. Any such change is effective upon posting on the Website. You should visit this page periodically to review any changes to the Privacy Policy.  Your use of the Website or the LogFrog app after such posting will constitute acceptance by you of such changes. If we make material changes in the way we collect, use, or share your personal information, we will notify you via the app and/or by prominently posting notice of the changes on our website.
